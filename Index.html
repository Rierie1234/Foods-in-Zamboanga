Unique Delicasies of Zamboanga


Welcome! Explore Zamboanga's culinary scene with its signature dishes like Curacha, a local crab delicacy, and Satti, a spicy skewered meat dish. Indulge in Tamal, a flavorful rice cake, and cool off with Knicker Bucker, a vibrant shaved ice dessert. Each bite is a celebration of tradition and taste

Curacha

Curacha, the majestic "king of crustaceans" from the Philippines, offers succulent, sweet meat that's a true delight for seafood lovers. Its unique appearance and tantalizing flavor make every bite an indulgent experience straight from the ocean's depths.

Tamal

Tamal, a culinary masterpiece wrapped in a corn husk, promises a symphony of flavors and textures. With each bite, you embark on a journey of tender masa enveloping a tantalizing filling, whether it's savory with succulent meats or sweet with decadent fruits. Its humble exterior belies the complex harmony of spices and ingredients within, making it a delightful indulgence for any palate.


Knickerbocker

Knickerbocker, a delicious fusion of flavors straight from the heart of Zamboanga! Picture layers of creamy avocado, sweetened saba bananas, and vibrant jelly, all topped with a scoop of rich ice cream and a sprinkle of crunchy peanuts. This local delight tantalizes the taste buds with every spoonful, offering a delightful symphony of textures and tastes that celebrate the culinary diversity of Zamboanga.



Satti

Satti is a mouthwatering dish originating from the Philippines particularly in Zamboanga, featuring skewered meat, often marinated in a savory blend of spices, and grilled to perfection. The aroma of the sizzling meat, combined with the charred edges, creates an irresistible temptation that tantalizes the taste buds. With each bite, you experience a symphony of flavors, from the smoky essence of the grill to the rich, succulent taste of the meat, making satti an absolute delight for the senses.
